FAQ

What services does Celebr8 offer?

Celebr8 offers comprehensive event management services, including wedding planning, corporate events, birthday celebrations, concerts, reunions, cultural programs, and conferences. We handle everything from venue selection to vendor coordination and on-site execution.

How do I book an event with Celebr8?

Booking an event with Celebr8 is super simple! Just reach out to us through our website or contact number. Our team will guide you through available packages, customization options, and all the steps needed to lock in your perfect date.

Can I customize the event packages?

Absolutely! Celebr8 offers a range of flexible packages that can be fully tailored to your needs, whether it’s decor, food, themes, or venue preferences. Your vision, our execution!

Does Celebr8 provide vendors or can I bring my own?

We have a trusted network of top-tier vendors, but if you have your own preferences, we’re happy to work with them as well. Flexibility is one of our core strengths.

What types of events does Celebr8 specialize in?

Celebr8 specializes in all types of events — from lavish weddings, product launches, and award ceremonies to fun birthdays, college fests, and intimate family get-togethers. No event is too big or too small for us.

How early should I book my event?

We recommend booking your event 4 to 6 weeks in advance to ensure everything is well organized. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings, depending on availability.
